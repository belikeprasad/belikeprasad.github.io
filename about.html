Our History

We star ted our operations in 2001 to provide hardware solutions to all our customers in and around Satara, Mahabaleshwar, Pune & karad also. Since then we have received over whelming response and till date we have more than 2000 machines that we have installed at our customers. We took up the dealership of Zenith Computers and our association with Zenith is now 7 years old. In this company we have provided customized solutions for Computers, Printers, Scanners, Un-interrupted power supply solutions as well as stabilizers. Networking is our speciality and we have more than 500 networked installations that we have installed and are still supporting. Our networking is independent of the Operating systems platform. We have installed and successfully implemented networks based on Windows 95/98, XP,windows 7/8/10, Windows Server o/s and Novell Netware 4.11/5.0. We also have installations where we have done networking of all the above platforms in the same network.

As the technology changes we have to change and we are proud that we have all the expertise available today which is state of art. .

We started a new firm in the name of G K SYSYTEMS PVT LTD. Also opened a new Servicing centre at our own place which is 550 sq ft where we provides a servicing & repairing of all types of machines, motherboard & all parts of the machines.
